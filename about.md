## About me
I am a PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Rochester. My research focuses on language documentation, description, and NLP for low-resourced languages.

## Research interests
- Language Documentation (Nigerian Languages)
- Negation–Tense–Aspect interactions (DM)
- Low-resource ASR (Whisper, wav2vec 2.0)
- Fieldwork methods and archiving (ELAN/BOLD/Saymore/)
